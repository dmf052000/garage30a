## HOW TO CREATE THIS BROCHURE IN CANVA

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. **Go to Canva.com**
   - Create free account or log in
   - Search "Real Estate Brochure"

2. **Choose Template**
   - Look for modern, luxury designs
   - Recommended: 8-page brochure or trifold
   - Dark themes work well for automotive

3. **Customize Design**
   - Replace template text with content above
   - Upload your photos from /public/images/
   - Use your logo.svg as header
   - Keep it clean and spacious

4. **Color Scheme**
   - Primary: Black (#000000)
   - Secondary: White (#FFFFFF)
   - Accent: Amber/Gold (#f59e0b)
   - Use sparingly for premium look

5. **Typography**
   - Headlines: Bold, large, clean
   - Body: Light, readable (12-14pt)
   - Keep it minimal and luxurious

6. **Export**
   - Download as PDF (High Quality)
   - Name: "Garage-30A-Brochure.pdf"
   - Save to /public/ folder

## TIPS FOR SUCCESS

### Design Tips:
- Use high-quality images only
- Lots of white/negative space
- Consistent fonts (max 2-3 font families)
- Professional, luxury feel
- Easy to scan/read quickly

### Content Tips:
- Focus on benefits, not just features
- Include specific numbers (18 units left)
- Show pricing or "Contact for Pricing"
- Multiple calls-to-action
- Contact info on every page (footer)

### Print Tips:
- If printing physical copies:
  - Use thick, premium paper (100lb+ cover stock)
  - Matte or soft-touch finish
  - Professional printer (not home printer)
  - Order 50-100 for events/tours
